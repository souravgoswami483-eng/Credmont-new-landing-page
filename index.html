Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to some of the most common questions about admissions, curriculum, and facilities.

What is the curriculum followed at Credmont?

We follow a competency-driven curriculum that aligns with international standards, India's National Education Policy (NEP 2020), and the National Curriculum Framework (NCF 2023). It is completely integrated spanning academics, skills, and activities.

What is the student-teacher ratio?

We maintain an excellent student-teacher ratio of 1:15 to ensure personalized attention and optimal mentorship for every child.

Does the school offer transportation?

Yes, we provide GPS-enabled transport facilities spanning across major routes in Howrah and surrounding areas to ensure your child's safety and convenience.

What extracurricular activities do you offer?

We boast over 35+ extracurricular activities and 25 student clubs, encompassing sports, arts, technology (STREAM lab), language clubs, and leadership programs like Model UN.
